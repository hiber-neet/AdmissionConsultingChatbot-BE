FPTU Admission Guide 2024

Welcome to FPT University Admission Process!

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about applying to FPTU:

1. Eligibility Requirements
   - High school graduation certificate
   - Minimum GPA of 6.0/10 or equivalent
   - English proficiency: IELTS 5.5+ or equivalent

2. Application Process
   - Online application submission
   - Document verification
   - Entrance exam or portfolio review
   - Interview (for selected programs)

3. Important Dates
   - Application period: March - July
   - Entrance exams: August
   - Results announcement: Late August
   - Enrollment: September

4. Required Documents
   - Application form
   - High school transcripts
   - ID card/Passport copy
   - English certificate
   - 4 passport photos

For more information, visit: www.fptu.edu.vn/admission
Contact: admission@fptu.edu.vn
