FPTU Tuition Fee Structure 2024-2025

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS:
- Software Engineering: 32,000,000 VND/semester
- Information Security: 32,000,000 VND/semester
- Artificial Intelligence: 35,000,000 VND/semester
- Business Administration: 28,000,000 VND/semester
- Digital Marketing: 26,000,000 VND/semester
- Graphic Design: 27,000,000 VND/semester

ADDITIONAL FEES:
- Enrollment fee (one-time): 3,000,000 VND
- Library fee (per semester): 200,000 VND
- Student insurance (per year): 150,000 VND
- Lab fees: varies by course

PAYMENT METHODS:
- Bank transfer
- Credit/Debit card
- Payment at cashier
- Installment plans available

PAYMENT DEADLINES:
- First installment: 2 weeks after enrollment
- Second installment: Mid-semester

For financial assistance inquiries, contact: finance@fptu.edu.vn
